Digital Preservation at the National Library of Norway

2024-05-29

Presentation at the Digital libraries: storage for now and forever conferenence hosted by the IFLA Preservation and Conservation Center at the National Library of Poland. The presentation is a crash course through digital storage and digital preservation practices at the National Library of Norway over the past 20 years. It concludes with current status and our future plans.
